# Question

The following chemical processes exist:

1. Zirconium nitride (ZrN) is a relatively inert solid material, but it can dissolve in hot, concentrated sulfuric acid to form a 1:1 type ionic compound, releasing only a simple gaseous substance during the dissolution process.  
2. Under a nitrogen atmosphere, a mixed solid of  $\mathrm{B}_2\mathrm{O}_3$  and  $\mathrm{CaB_6}$  is co-heated, producing only two binary compounds, one of which can be used as a wear-resistant material.  
3. Treating  $\mathrm{Tb}_{4} \mathrm{O}_{7}$  with  $0.1 \mathrm{~mol} / \mathrm{L}$  dilute hydrochloric acid can produce pure  $\mathrm{TbO}_{2}$ .  
4. Magnetite is added to an excess of  $\mathrm{K}_2\mathrm{Cr}_2\mathrm{O}_7 - \mathrm{H}_2\mathrm{SO}_4$  solution to dissolve it.  
5. In an alkaline sodium hydroxide solution, nickel sulfide reacts quantitatively with carbon monoxide in a molar ratio of  $1:5$ .

Given that the stoichiometric coefficients are balanced as integer ratios, the correct option regarding the chemical equations involved in the above five processes is:

A. All other options are incorrect  
B. The sum of reactant coefficients for Process 1 is 8.  
C. The reactant coefficients of Process 2 are all less than 10.  
D. The sum of product coefficients in Process 3 is greater than the sum of reactant coefficients in Process 1.  
E. The absolute value of the difference between the sum of reactant coefficients and the sum of product coefficients in Process 4 is a prime number.

F. The ratio of the sum of reactant coefficients to the sum of product coefficients in Process 5 is a decimal.

# Answer

Correct Answer: A

# Detailed Explanation

Therefore, the product is  $\mathrm{ZrOSO}_4$ , which conforms to a 1:1 type ionic compound. Only elemental gas is released, which can only be hydrogen gas. Balancing the equation:
