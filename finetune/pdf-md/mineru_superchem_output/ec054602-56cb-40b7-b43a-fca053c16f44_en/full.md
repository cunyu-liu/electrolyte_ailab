# Question

Given the equilibrium constants (concentration equilibrium constants, units omitted) for the reaction of Xe with  $\mathrm{F}_2$  at  $500^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$ :

$$
\mathrm {X e} + \mathrm {F} _ {2} = \mathrm {X e F} _ {2} \quad \mathrm {K} _ {1} = 2 9. 8
$$

$$
\mathrm {X e F} _ {2} + \mathrm {F} _ {2} = \mathrm {X e F} _ {4} \quad \mathrm {K} _ {2} = 0. 5 0
$$

$$
\mathrm {X e F} _ {4} + \mathrm {F} _ {2} = \mathrm {X e F} _ {6} \quad \mathrm {K} _ {3} = 3. 3 0 \times 1 0 ^ {- 3}
$$

At  $500^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$ , Xe at an initial concentration of  $0.100\mathrm{mol/L}$  and  $\mathrm{F}_2$  at an initial concentration of  $0.200\mathrm{mol/L}$  react in a constant-volume container. What is the remaining concentration of  $\mathrm{F}_2$  when the reaction reaches equilibrium? (Retain two significant figures in the result)

A. All other options are incorrect  
B. 0.070  
C. 0.080  
D. 0.090  
E. 0.10  
F. 0.11  
G. 0.12  
H. 0.13

1. 0.14

# Answer

Correct Answer: G

# Detailed Explanation

Let the equilibrium concentration of Xe be  $x$ , the concentration of  $\mathrm{F}_2$  be  $y$ , and the concentrations of  $\mathrm{XeF}_2$ ,  $\mathrm{XeF}_4$ ,  $\mathrm{XeF}_6$  be  $a$ ,  $b$ ,  $c$  respectively, then:

$$
\begin{array}{l} x + a + b + c = 0. 1 \\ y + a + 2 b + 3 c = 0. 2 \\ a = 2 9. 8 x y \\ \end{array}
$$

# CHECKPOINT

1 PTS

$$
a = 2 9. 8 x y
$$

$$
b = 0. 5 0 a y = 2 9. 8 \times 0. 5 0 x y ^ {2}
$$

# CHECKPOINT

1 PTS

$$
b = 2 9. 8 \times 0. 5 0 x y ^ {2}
$$

$$
c = 3. 3 0 \times 1 0 ^ {- 3} b y = 3. 3 0 \times 1 0 ^ {- 3} \times 2 9. 8 \times 0. 5 0 x y ^ {3}
$$

# CHECKPOINT

1 PTS

$$
c = 3. 3 0 \times 1 0 ^ {- 3} \times 2 9. 8 \times 0. 5 0 x y ^ {3}
$$

Substituting the last three equations into the first two equations, we have:

$$
x \left(1 + 2 9. 8 y + 1 4. 9 y ^ {2} + 0. 0 4 9 2 y ^ {3}\right) = 0. 1
$$

$$
y \big (1 + 2 9. 8 x + 2 9. 8 x y + 0. 1 4 7 6 x y ^ {2} \big) = 0. 2
$$

The first equation is transformed into  $x = \frac{0.1}{1 + 29.8y + 14.9y^2 + 0.0492y^3} = \frac{\frac{0.2}{y} - 1}{29.8 + 29.8y + 0.1476y^2}$

# CHECKPOINT

$$
x = \frac {\frac {0 . 2}{y} - 1}{2 9 . 8 + 2 9 . 8 y + 0 . 1 4 7 6 y ^ {2}}
$$

Substituting  $x$  into the second equation, we obtain  $y = 0.117$
