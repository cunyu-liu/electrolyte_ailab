# Question

When sodium carbonate is added to a mineral containing element A and burned with oxygen, a sodium salt B containing A is obtained. After acidification and evaporative crystallization of B, colorless crystals C are formed. C readily dehydrates to yield a white solid D. When D reacts with  $\mathrm{SF}_4$  above  $110^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$ , only gas E containing A with a mass fraction of  $50.96\%$  and another gas F are produced. F reacts with an aqueous solution of C to precipitate red crystals G. When G reacts with  $\mathrm{SbF}_5$  at  $-23^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$  in  $\mathrm{SO}_2$  in a 1:5 ratio, an ionic compound H and  $\mathrm{SbF}_3$  are formed in a 1:1 product ratio. Which of the following statements is correct:

A. The element  $\mathbf{A}$  in  $\mathbf{B}$  is in its highest oxidation state  
B. In the infinite chain structure of  $\mathbf{D}$ , there are three chemically distinct oxygen atoms.  
C. The point group of molecule  $\mathbf{E}$  is the same as that of  $\mathrm{CCl}_4$ .  
D. The element  $\mathbf{A}$  in  $\mathbf{G}$  is in its lowest valence state  
E. In the cation of  $\mathbf{H}$ , the  $\mathbf{A}$  atom has two coordination numbers.  
F. There is a fourfold rotation axis for the anions in  $\mathbf{H}$

# Answer

Correct Answer: E

# Detailed Explanation

The key breakthrough point of the question lies in the gas  $\mathbf{E}$ .  $\mathbf{D}$  reacts with  $\mathrm{SF}_4$  to produce gas  $\mathbf{E}$  and gas  $\mathbf{F}$ .  $\mathrm{SF}_4$  is a commonly used fluorinating agent that can replace oxygen atoms in oxides with fluorine atoms. Therefore,  $\mathbf{D}$  is an oxide of  $\mathbf{A}$ , and  $\mathbf{E}$  is a fluoride of  $\mathbf{A}$ . The mass fraction of  $\mathbf{A}$  in  $\mathbf{E}$  is  $50.96\%$ , the atomic weight of  $\mathbf{A}$  is  $\mathbf{M}$ , and the chemical formula of  $\mathbf{E}$  is Double subscripts: use braces to clarify (the atomic weight of  $\mathbf{F}$  is 19). Then we have:  $\frac{xM}{xM + 19y} = 0.5096$ . By trial and error, we can obtain the most suitable result: element  $\mathbf{A}$  is Se, and  $\mathbf{E}$  is  $\mathrm{SeF}_4$ .
