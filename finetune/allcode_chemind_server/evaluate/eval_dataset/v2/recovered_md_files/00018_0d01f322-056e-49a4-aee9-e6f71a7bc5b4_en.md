# Answer

Correct Answer: M

# Detailed Explanation

Step one: The high temperature represents typical conditions for a pericyclic reaction. Observing the molecular structure, it can be found that the furan ring can act as a diene, while the terminal double bond can act as a dienophile. The two undergo a D-A reaction to generate the polycyclic intermediate shown in Figure 2:

![](images/49c9c2333e5826630ba1efc44ad97bd767e52cc342e3da38e94f7441dbb9fc6e.jpg)  
Fig. 2, the molecule in the figure is described by SMILES as: CC1(C=C2)OC32N(S(=O)(C(F)(F)F)=O)CCC3C1
