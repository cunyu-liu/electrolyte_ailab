# Answer

Correct Answer: G

# Detailed Explanation

Based on the prompt in the question stem, "four cobalt atoms and four oxygen atoms form a cubane-like structural core," it can be inferred that A contains four cobalt atoms. Therefore, the relative molecular weight of A is  $\frac{4 \times 58.93}{0.2766} = 852.21 \mathrm{~g/mol}$ .

According to the preparation method, the ligands in  $\mathbf{A}$  are acetate ions and pyridine (abbreviated as py). Let the number of acetate ions be  $x$  and the number of pyridine molecules be  $y$ . Solving the indeterminate equation  $4 \times (58.93 + 16) + 59.04x + 79.1y = 852.21$ , where  $x$  and  $y$  are integers, we find that  $x = 4$ ,  $y = 4$  is a reasonable solution. Thus, the structure of  $\mathbf{A}$  is  $\mathrm{Co}_4\mathrm{O}_4(\mathrm{CH}_3\mathrm{COO})_4(\mathrm{py})_4$ .
