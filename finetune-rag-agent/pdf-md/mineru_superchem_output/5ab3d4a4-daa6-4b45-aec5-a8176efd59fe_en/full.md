# Question

The researchers reacted  $\mathrm{ZrCl}_4$ ,  $\mathrm{Me}_3\mathrm{PNSiMe}_3$ , and  $\mathrm{NaF}$  at  $220^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$ , followed by crystallization in dichloromethane, to obtain a  $\mathrm{Zr(IV)}$  complex.
