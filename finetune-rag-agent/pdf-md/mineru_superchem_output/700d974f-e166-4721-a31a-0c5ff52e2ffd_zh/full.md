首先计算二价铜还原到一价铜的标准电势：

$$
\varphi_ {\mathrm {B}} ^ {\theta} \left(\mathrm {C u} ^ {2 +} / \mathrm {C u} ^ {+}\right) = 2 \varphi^ {\theta} \left(\mathrm {C u} ^ {2 +} / \mathrm {C u}\right) - \varphi^ {\theta} \left(\mathrm {C u} ^ {+} / \mathrm {C u}\right) = 0. 1 6 3 \mathrm {V}
$$

$$
