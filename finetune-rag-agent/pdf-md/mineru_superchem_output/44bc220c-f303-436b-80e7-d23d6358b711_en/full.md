# Question

$1 \times 10^{-3} \mathrm{~mol}$  of  $\mathrm{Cd(CN)}_{2}$  is placed in  $1 \mathrm{~L}$  of buffer solution at  $\mathrm{pH} = 8.5$ . It is known that the cumulative stability constants of  $\mathrm{CN}^{-}$ to  $\mathrm{Cd}^{2+}$  are  $10^{5.48}, 10^{10.60}, 10^{15.23}, 10^{18.78}$ , respectively, and the solubility product constant of  $\mathrm{Cd(OH)}_{2}$  is  $K_{\mathrm{sp}} = 4 \times 10^{-13}$ , and the dissociation constant of HCN is  $K_{\mathrm{a}} = 6.2 \times 10^{-10}$ . Coordination of cadmium species and  $\mathrm{OH}^{-}$ is ignored.

The following statements are made:

1. The species conservation of cyanide ions in the solution is

$$
\left[ \mathrm {C N} ^ {-} \right] + \left[ \mathrm {H C N} \right] + \left[ \mathrm {C d} (\mathrm {C N}) ^ {+} \right] + 2 \left[ \mathrm {C d} (\mathrm {C N}) _ {2} \right] + 3 \left[ \mathrm {C d} (\mathrm {C N}) _ {3} ^ {-} \right] + 4 \left[ \mathrm {C d} (\mathrm {C N}) _ {4} ^ {2 -} \right] = 2 \times 1 0 ^ {- 3} \mathrm {m o l} / \mathrm {L}
$$

2. The charge conservation in the solution is

$$
2 \left[ \mathrm {C d} ^ {2 +} \right] + \left[ \mathrm {C d} (\mathrm {C N}) ^ {+} \right] + \left[ \mathrm {H} ^ {+} \right] = \left[ \mathrm {C d} (\mathrm {C N}) _ {3} ^ {-} \right] + 2 \left[ \mathrm {C d} (\mathrm {C N}) _ {4} ^ {2 -} \right] + \left[ \mathrm {C N} ^ {-} \right] + \left[ \mathrm {O H} ^ {-} \right]
$$

3. Let  $\left[\mathrm{CN}^{-}\right] = a\cdot b\times 10^{-c}\mathrm{mol / L}$  (retain two significant figures), then  $a + b + c = 10$  
4. The system will produce  $\mathrm{Cd(OH)}_2$  precipitate

What is the sum of the serial numbers of all correct statements?

A. 1  
B. 3  
C. 4  
D. 6  
E. 7  
F. 8

G. All of the above options are incorrect.

# Answer

Correct Answer: C

# Detailed Explanation

This question cannot be solved using charge conservation because the buffer ion concentration changes before and after the addition of  $\mathrm{Cd(CN)}_2$ , its relative buffer ion concentration is small, but the relative concentrations of cadmium-containing and cyanide-containing species are large and unknown. Statement 1 regarding material balance is correct, and statement 2 regarding charge conservation is incorrect because buffer ions are not considered.
