# Question

The nitrate of metal  $\mathbf{M}$  reacts with 1,4-benzenedicarboxylic acid (abbreviated as  $\mathrm{H}_2\mathrm{BDC}$ ) in a mixed system of N,N-dimethylformamide (abbreviated as DMF) and anhydrous ethanol to yield a valuable material  $\mathbf{A}$ . Single-crystal X-ray diffraction analysis of  $\mathbf{A}$  reveals that  $\mathbf{A}$  belongs to the orthorhombic crystal system, with unit cell parameters  $a = 1923.01$  pm,  $b = 723.87$  pm,  $c = 884.82$  pm,  $Z = 4$ , and a density of  $\rho = 1.597~\mathrm{g / cm^3}$ .

The framework structure of  $\mathbf{A}$  primarily consists of  $\mathbf{M}$  and  $\mathrm{BDC}^{2-}$ , with solvent molecules incorporated in the structure. Elemental analysis indicates that  $\mathbf{A}$  contains five elements:  $\mathbf{M}$ ,  $\mathbf{C}$ ,  $\mathbf{H}$ ,  $\mathbf{O}$ , and  $\mathbf{N}$ , with an atomic ratio of  $\mathbf{M}$  to  $\mathbf{C}$  of 1:11. When  $\mathbf{A}$  is subjected to high-temperature oxidation in air, nanoparticles of a common oxide of  $\mathbf{M}$  are obtained, and the mass fraction of  $\mathbf{M}$  in this common oxide is approximately  $70\%$ .

Based on the above information, determine  $\mathbf{M}$ .

A. Co  
B. Ni  
C. Cu  
D. Ca  
E. Fe  
F. Na

# Answer

Correct Answer: A
