# Question

The solutions obtained by dissolving A, B, and C in water all have a certain alkalinity. Which of the following options is correct?

A. All other options are incorrect  
B. A mixed gas does not necessarily contain  $H_{2}$ .  
C. Both gases released during thermogravimetric analysis are flammable.  
D. If the reaction is carried out in an air atmosphere, the temperature at which C is formed will be higher.
