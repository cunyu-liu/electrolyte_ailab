# Question

$$

Excess  $\mathrm{PbCrO_4(s)}$  is added to pure water, and solution equilibrium is reached.

Please calculate the following questions in order:

At this time, the relationship between the amount of substance of  $\mathrm{Pb(OH)_2(s)}$  and the volume of water satisfies a direct proportional function  $n = aV$ , where  $n$  is the amount of substance (in units of mol), and  $V$  is the volume of water (in units of L). Give the coefficient  $a$  (no need to give units).

The following options give possible answers to questions 1, 2, and 3 in order. The units have already been given in the questions. Choose the closest option.

# Answer

Correct Answer: C

$$
[ H ^ {+} ] + 2 [ P b ^ {2 +} ] = [ O H ^ {-} ] + [ H C r O _ {4} ^ {-} ] + 2 [ C r O _ {4} ^ {2 -} ] + 2 [ C r _ {2} O _ {7} ^ {2 -} ]

$$

$$
