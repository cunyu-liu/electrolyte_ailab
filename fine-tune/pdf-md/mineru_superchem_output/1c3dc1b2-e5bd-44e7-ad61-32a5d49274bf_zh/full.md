# 题目

$\mathrm{Fe}^{3+}$  水解过程中产生  $\mathrm{FeOH}^{2+}$  和  $\mathrm{Fe}_2(\mathrm{OH})_2^{4+}$ , 为研究  $\mathrm{Fe}_2(\mathrm{OH})_2^{4+}$  的解聚, 并提出了以下的反应机理:

$$
\mathrm {F e} _ {2} (\mathrm {O H}) _ {2} ^ {4 +} \backslash \mathrm {x r i g h t l e f t h a r p o o n s} [ k _ {- 1} ] k _ {1} 2 \mathrm {F e O H} ^ {2 +} \mathrm {F e} _ {2} (\mathrm {O H}) _ {2} ^ {4 +} + \mathrm {H} ^ {+} \backslash \mathrm {x r i g h t l e f t h a r p o o n s} [ k _ {- 2} ] k _ {2} \mathrm {F e} ^ {3 +} + \mathrm {F e O H} ^ {2 +} + \mathrm {H} _ {2} \mathrm {O F e} ^ {3 +} + \mathrm {H} _ {2} \mathrm {O} \rightleftharpoons \mathrm {F e O H} ^ {2 +} + \mathrm {H} ^ {+},
$$

前两个方程为基元反应, 第三个为平衡方程, 推导用  $k_{1}, k_{-1}, k_{2}, k_{-2}, c_{H}$  和  $\beta$  表示的表观水解平衡常数  $K = \frac{c_{1}^{2}}{c_{2}}$ . 其中  $c_{H}$  为水溶液中氢离子浓度,  $c_{1}$  为单核离子总浓度,  $c_{2}$  为二聚体  $\mathrm{Fe}_{2}(\mathrm{OH})_{2}^{4+}$  的浓度, 水的浓度已经包含在各个常数中, 不必标出.

A.  $K = \frac{k_{1} + k_{2}c_{H}}{\frac{c_{H}}{c_{H} + \beta}\left(k_{-1}\frac{c_{H}}{c_{H} + \beta} + k_{-2}\frac{c_{H}}{c_{H} + \beta}\right)}$  
B.  $K = \frac{k_{1} + k_{2}c_{H}}{k_{-1}\frac{c_{H}}{c_{H} + \beta} + k_{-2}\frac{\beta}{c_{H} + \beta}}$  
C.  $K = \frac{k_{1} + k_{2}c_{H}}{\frac{c_{H}}{c_{H} + \beta}(k_{-1} + k_{-2})}$  
D.  $K = \frac{\left(k_{1} + k_{2}c_{H}\right)\left(c_{H} + \beta\right)^{2}}{k_{-1}c_{H}^{2}}$  
E.  $K = \frac{(k_{1} + k_{2}c_{H})(c_{H} + \beta)^{2}}{k_{-2}c_{H}\beta}$  
F.  $K = \frac{k_{1} + k_{2}c_{H}}{\frac{\beta}{c_{H} + \beta}\left(k_{-1}\frac{c_{H}}{c_{H} + \beta} + k_{-2}\frac{\beta}{c_{H} + \beta}\right)}$  
G.  $K = \frac{k_{1} + k_{2}c_{H}}{\frac{\beta}{c_{H} + \beta}\left(k_{-1}\frac{\beta}{c_{H} + \beta} + k_{-2}\frac{c_{H}}{c_{H} + \beta}\right)}$

# 答案

正确答案: G

# 详细解析

第一个反应解聚的速率为  $v_{d1} = k_1c_2$

# CHECKPOINT

$$
v _ {d 1} = k _ {1} c _ {2}
$$

1 PTS

第二个反应解聚的速率为  $v_{d2} = k_2c_2c_H$

# CHECKPOINT

$$
v _ {d 2} = k _ {2} c _ {2} c _ {H}
$$

1 PTS

总解聚速率  $v_{d} = v_{d1} + v_{d2} = (k_{1} + k_{2}c_{H})c_{2}$

# CHECKPOINT

$$
v _ {d} = \left(k _ {1} + k _ {2} c _ {H}\right) c _ {2}
$$

0.5 PTS

$$
c \left(\mathrm {F e} ^ {3 +}\right) = c _ {1} \frac {c _ {H}}{c _ {H} + \beta} c \left(\mathrm {F e O H} ^ {2 +}\right) = c _ {1} \frac {\beta}{c _ {H} + \beta}
$$

利用分布系数推导单核形态平衡浓度：

# CHECKPOINT

$$
c \left(\mathrm {F e} ^ {3 +}\right) = c _ {1} \frac {c _ {H}}{c _ {H} + \beta}
$$

0.5 PTS

# CHECKPOINT

0.5 PTS

$$
c \left(\mathrm {F e O H} ^ {2 +}\right) = c _ {1} \frac {\beta}{c _ {H} + \beta}
$$

第一个反应聚合速率为  $v_{a1} = k_{-1}c\big(\mathrm{FeOH}^{2 + }\big)^{2} = k_{-1}c_{1}^{2}\Big(\frac{\beta}{c_{H} + \beta}\Big)^{2}$

# CHECKPOINT

1 PTS

$$
v _ {a 1} = k _ {- 1} c _ {1} ^ {2} \left(\frac {\beta}{c _ {H} + \beta}\right) ^ {2}
$$

第二个反应聚合速率为  $v_{a2} = k_{-2}c\left(\mathrm{Fe}^{3 + }\right)c\left(\mathrm{FeOH}^{2 + }\right) = k_{-2}c_1^2\frac{c_H}{c_H + \beta}\frac{\beta}{c_H + \beta}$

# CHECKPOINT

1 PTS

$$
v _ {a 2} = k _ {- 2} c _ {2} ^ {1} \frac {c _ {H}}{e H + \beta} \frac {\beta}{e H + \beta}
$$

总聚合速率  $v_{a} = v_{a1} + v_{a2} = \left(k_{-1}\left(\frac{\beta}{c_{H} + \beta}\right)^{2} + k_{-2}\frac{c_{H}}{c_{H} + \beta}\frac{\beta}{c_{H} + \beta}\right)c_{1}^{2}$

# CHECKPOINT

0.5 PTS

$$
v _ {a} = \left(k _ {- 1} \left(\frac {\beta}{c _ {H} + \beta}\right) ^ {2} + k _ {- 2} \frac {c _ {H}}{c _ {H} + \beta} \frac {\beta}{c _ {H} + \beta}\right) c _ {1} ^ {2}
$$

平衡时，二聚体总解聚反应速率  $v_{d}$  与总聚合反应速率  $v_{a}$  满足  $v_{d} = v_{a}$ ，即

# CHECKPOINT

1 PTS

$$
v _ {d} = v _ {a}
$$

$$
(k _ {1} + k _ {2} c _ {H}) c _ {2} = \left(k _ {- 1} \left(\frac {\beta}{c _ {H} + \beta}\right) ^ {2} + k _ {- 2} \frac {c _ {H}}{c _ {H} + \beta} \frac {\beta}{c _ {H} + \beta}\right) c _ {1} ^ {2}
$$

变形得

$$
K = \frac {k _ {1} + k _ {2} c _ {H}}{\frac {\beta}{c _ {H} + \beta} \left(k _ {- 1} \frac {\beta}{c _ {H} + \beta} + k _ {- 2} \frac {c _ {H}}{c _ {H} + \beta}\right)}
$$

# CHECKPOINT
