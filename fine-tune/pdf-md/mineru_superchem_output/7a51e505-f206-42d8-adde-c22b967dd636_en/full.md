If the cation and anion are treated as free ions, all  $\mathrm{Re}$  atoms exhibit identical chemical environments.  $\mathbf{B}$  decomposes above  $-20^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$  to produce a Re-containing compound  $\mathbf{C}$ . This reaction yields no ionic compounds, and no  $\mathrm{Xe} - \mathrm{O}$  bonds are present in the products. Throughout these processes, the oxidation state of the metal  $\mathrm{Re}$  remains unchanged. In this context, if HF is generated as a reaction product, it is not considered a gas.

Which of the following statements is correct?

A. The relative molecular mass of A is 292.2  
B. The reaction producing B and the reaction producing C have inconsistent gaseous products.  
C. The chemical equation for the formation of  $\mathbf{B}$  (with coefficients in the simplest ratio), where the sum of the product coefficients is an odd number.  
D. The chemical equation for the formation of C (with coefficients in the simplest ratio), where the sum of the product coefficients is an odd number.  
E. The cationic structure of  $\mathbf{B}$  is trigonal.  
F. The anionic structure of  $\mathbf{B}$  exhibits fourfold rotational symmetry.  
G. In the chemical equation for the formation of  $\mathbf{C}$  (with coefficients in the simplest whole-number ratio), the coefficient of  $\mathbf{C}$  is 4.

H. Among the elements that constitute  $\mathbf{C}$ , there are two elements with the same atomic number.
