# Question

The wastewater produced during copper smelting contains a high concentration of arsenic, mainly in the form of  $AsO_{2}^{-}$ . The following treatments were all carried out at room temperature.

First, add a  $26\%$ $Na_2S$  solution, adjust the  $pH$  to 0.8 with sulfuric acid, and after reacting for twenty minutes, a precipitate is obtained, which is recorded as reaction 1.

Subsequently, the precipitate is added to a slightly excessive amount of  $NaOH$  solution at a molar ratio of 1:7 to ensure complete dissolution of the precipitate, which is recorded as reaction 2.

After dissolution, a small amount of hydroquinone is added as a catalyst and sodium dodecyl sulfonate as a surfactant, and air is passed through for ten hours for oxidation and desulfurization, which is recorded as reaction 3.

After oxidation, the  $pH$  is adjusted to 0 with sulfuric acid, and  $SO_2$  is then passed through to generate a precipitate, which is recorded as reaction 4.

Finally, the precipitate is filtered to obtain a solid oxide M with high purity. Detection reveals that the final remaining filtrate still contains a certain amount of  $Re$  element that can be recovered.

The above filtrate is extracted with an organic phase composed of  $20\%$  trioctylamine,  $40\%$  sec-octyl alcohol, and  $40\%$  kerosene. After liquid separation, the organic phase is taken and extracted with ammonia water. After liquid separation again, the aqueous phase is frozen, crystallized, and separated to obtain a pure compound  $A$  without water of crystallization.

1.3874g of compound  $A$  is taken and gradually heated to  $800^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$  in a  $H_{2}$  atmosphere, and the final solid product  $B$  is 0.9631g Re, and 0.3727g of water vapor is separated in the tail gas. If heated in an air atmosphere, 100% weight loss is observed at  $400^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$ .

Regarding the above process, the following descriptions are given. Let the product of the serial numbers of all incorrect descriptions be  $x$ , and the sum of the serial numbers of all correct descriptions be  $y$ . Find  $\frac{x}{(y + 10)^2}$  and select the option with an absolute error  $< 0.001$ . If there is no option that meets the condition, select  $A$ .

1. It is not possible to select four coefficients from the equation for reaction 1 that form an arithmetic sequence.  
2. The sum of the coefficients on the left side of the equation for reaction 2 divided by the sum of the coefficients on the right side is equal to 1.4.  
3. The average coefficient of the equation for reaction 3 is equal to 3.8.  
4. The product of all the coefficients on both sides of the equation for reaction 4 is equal to 144.  
5. The catalytic principle of hydroquinone is the generation of a p-benzoquinone intermediate.

6. One molecule of compound  $A$  contains 10 atoms.  
7. One molecule of compound  $A$  contains 9 atoms.  
8. When compound  $A$  is treated at  $800^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$  in a  $H_{2}$  atmosphere, one molecule of compound  $A$  generates 4 molecules of gas.  
9. When treated at  $400^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$  in an air atmosphere, a reaction with oxygen occurs.  
10. Organic phase extraction utilizes sec-octyl alcohol and kerosene to suppress the formation of a third phase.  
11. Back extraction cannot use a high  $pH$  solution.

A. All other options are incorrect

# Answer

Correct Answer: E

# Detailed Explanation

The equations for reactions 1 to 4 are as follows:

$$
2 A s O _ {2} ^ {-} + 3 S ^ {2 -} + 8 H ^ {+} \rightarrow A s _ {2} S _ {3} + 4 H _ {2} O
$$

$$
A s _ {2} S _ {3} + 6 O H ^ {-} \rightarrow A s O _ {3} ^ {3 -} + A s S _ {3} ^ {3 -} + 3 H _ {2} O
$$

$$
2 A s O _ {3} ^ {3 -} + 2 A s S _ {3} ^ {3 -} + 5 O _ {2} \rightarrow 4 A s O _ {4} ^ {3 -} + 6 S
$$

$$
2 A s O _ {4} ^ {3 -} + 2 S O _ {2} + 2 H ^ {+} \rightarrow A s _ {2} O _ {3} + 2 S O _ {4} ^ {2 -} + H _ {2} O
$$

The coefficients 1, 2, 3, 4 in the equation of reaction 1 constitute an arithmetic sequence with a common difference of 1, so statement 1 is incorrect.
