# Answer

Correct Answer: C

# Detailed Explanation

(1)

Let the final  $\left[\mathbf{F}^{-}\right] = \mathbf{x}\mathrm{mol}\cdot \mathbf{L}^{-1}$

$$
\left[ \mathrm {H F} \right] = \left(1 0 ^ {- 3. 5 0} / 1 0 ^ {- 3. 1 7}\right) \mathrm {x} = 0. 4 6 8 \mathrm {x m o l} \cdot \mathrm {L} ^ {- 1}
$$

$$
\left[ \mathrm {H F} _ {2} ^ {-} \right] = 3. 9 8 \cdot 0. 4 6 8 \mathrm {x} \cdot \mathrm {x} = 1. 8 6 \mathrm {x} ^ {2} \mathrm {m o l} \cdot \mathrm {L} ^ {- 1}
$$

Material balance:  $\left[\mathrm{F}^{-}\right] + \left[\mathrm{HF}\right] + 2\left[\mathrm{HF}_{2}^{-}\right] = (1 + 0.468)\mathrm{x} + 2 \cdot 1.86\mathrm{x}^{2} = 0.200(\mathrm{mol}\cdot \mathrm{L}^{-1})$

Solving for  $x = 0.107$ ,  $\left[\mathrm{F}^{-}\right] = 0.107\mathrm{mol}\cdot \mathrm{L}^{-1}$ ,  $\left[\mathrm{HF}\right] = 0.050\mathrm{mol}\cdot \mathrm{L}^{-1}$ ,  $\left[\mathrm{HF}_{2}^{-}\right] = 0.021\mathrm{mol}\cdot \mathrm{L}^{-1}$

$$

Ignoring  $\left[\mathrm{OH}^{-}\right]$ ,  $\left[\mathrm{K}^{+}\right] \approx \left[\mathrm{F}^{-}\right] + \left[\mathrm{HF}_{2}^{-}\right] - \left[\mathrm{H}^{+}\right] = 0.128 \, \mathrm{mol} \cdot \mathrm{L}^{-1}$ ,  $\mathrm{m(KOH)} = 0.72 \, \mathrm{g}$
