# Question

Fluorine gas reacts with hydrazoic acid to produce 3 products: a certain pseudohalogen-halogen interhalide, a gas, and a common salt. Which of the following statements is correct:

A. The generated pseudohalogen-halogen interhalide is  $\mathrm{N}_3\mathrm{F}_3$  
B. Gaseous product is ammonia.  
C. The stoichiometric ratio of fluorine gas to hydrazoic acid in this reaction may be 1:4  
D. The gaseous product is nitrogen trifluoride.  
E. For every mole of fluorine gas consumed in the reaction, 6 moles of gas may be produced.  
F. The anion in the salt produced by the reaction is  $\mathrm{N}_3^-$  
G. All other options are incorrect.

# Answer

Correct Answer: C

# Detailed Explanation

The only halogen in the question is fluorine, and the only pseudohalogen is azide, so the pseudohalogen-halogen interhalogen compound is fluorine azide.

# CHECKPOINT

1 PTS

The pseudohalogen-halogen interhalogen compound is  $\mathrm{FN}_3$

Azide is oxidized to nitrogen gas by strongly oxidizing fluorine, so the gas is nitrogen gas.

# CHECKPOINT

1 PTS

The gaseous product is  $\mathbf{N}_2$

The hydrogen element can only exist as ammonium ion, and the anion is the reduction product fluoride ion, so the salt is ammonium fluoride.

# CHECKPOINT

1 PTS

The salt product is  $\mathrm{NH}_4\mathrm{F}$

Therefore, options A, B, and D are all incorrect. Next, balance the equation. There are two reasonable equations:

$$
\mathrm {F} _ {2} + 4 \mathrm {H N} _ {3} \rightarrow \mathrm {F N} _ {3} + \mathrm {N H} _ {4} \mathrm {F} + 4 \mathrm {N} _ {2}
$$

$$
\mathrm {2 F _ {2} + 4 H N _ {3} \rightarrow 3 F N _ {3} + N H _ {4} F + N _ {2}}
$$

# CHECKPOINT

2 PTS

$$
\mathrm {F} _ {2} + 4 \mathrm {H N} _ {3} \rightarrow \mathrm {F N} _ {3} + \mathrm {N H} _ {4} \mathrm {F} + 4 \mathrm {N} _ {2}
$$

$$
2 \mathrm {F} _ {2} + 4 \mathrm {H N} _ {3} \rightarrow 3 \mathrm {F N} _ {3} + \mathrm {N H} _ {4} \mathrm {F} + \mathrm {N} _ {2}
$$

Fluorine azide is also a gas, so the stoichiometric ratio of fluorine gas to gas products is between 1:2 and 1:5.

E is incorrect.

The stoichiometric ratio of fluorine gas to hydrazoic acid is between 1:2 and 1:4.

The stoichiometric ratio of fluorine gas to hydrazoic acid is 1:2 to 1:4

C is correct.
