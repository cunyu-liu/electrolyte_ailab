# Question

S crystal contains three types of ions: tetrahedral  $\mathbf{A}$ ,  $\mathbf{B}$ , and heavy atom coordination polyhedron octahedral  $\mathbf{C}$ , in a ratio of  $1:1:1$ . It belongs to the orthorhombic crystal system, with cell parameters  $a = 6.9550\AA$ ,  $b = 6.1420\AA$ ,  $c = 11.2180\AA$ . There are two chemical formulas in one unit cell. Heating  $\mathbf{S}$  to  $100^{\circ}\mathrm{C}$  results in a weight loss of  $44.05\%$ , and further heating results in another weight loss of  $10.61\%$ , yielding a stable compound  $\mathbf{X}$ .  $\mathbf{X}$  contains  $27.83\%$  of the non-metallic element  $\mathbf{Y}$ . Which of the following statements are correct:

1. All elements in  $\mathbf{S}$  are macroelements in plants.  
2. The sum of all atoms in the chemical formula of  $\mathbf{X}$  is 12.  
3. From the transformation of  $\mathbf{S}$  to  $\mathbf{X}$ , 14 moles of gas are produced for every 1 mole of  $\mathbf{X}$  formed.  
4. The density of the crystal is  $1.701\mathrm{g/cm^3}$ .  
5. The stoichiometric ratio of each species in the second step heating chemical equation is 2:1:2:1.

A. All other options are incorrect  
B. 1.5.  
C. 1.2.4.  
D. 2.4.5.  
E. 3.4.  
F. 2.5.  
G. 1.4.5.

H. 2.3.5.

# Answer

Correct Answer: G

$$
